# blue_ocean_reframing

Purpose: Redefine a problem space by eliminating competition and assumptions.

## When To Use
- Solutions feel incremental
- Problem space is saturated
- Targeting moonshot innovation

## Inputs
- Existing solutions or approaches

## Process
1. List current solutions in the space
2. Apply ERRC grid:
   - **Eliminate** — what factors can be removed entirely?
   - **Reduce** — what can be scaled below industry standard?
   - **Raise** — what should be raised well above standard?
   - **Create** — what factors does the industry not offer?
3. Redefine value proposition based on grid
4. Identify new user experience that results

## Outputs
- Differentiated concept
- Completed ERRC grid
- New value curve

## Constraints
- Must challenge at least one core assumption
- Avoid incremental improvements
