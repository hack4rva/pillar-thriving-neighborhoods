# systems_mapping

Purpose: Map interconnected systems to identify leverage points for intervention.

## When To Use
- Problem spans multiple stakeholders
- Risk of unintended consequences
- Civic or infrastructure-heavy domains

## Inputs
- Problem domain
- Known actors (people, orgs, systems)

## Process
1. Identify actors: users, institutions, systems
2. Map flows between actors:
   - Data flows
   - Money flows
   - Decision flows
3. Identify bottlenecks and feedback loops
4. Select leverage point for intervention

## Outputs
- System map (diagram or structured list)
- Identified leverage point
- Targeted intervention strategy

## Constraints
- Keep map simple — no over-modeling
- Focus on actionable intervention, not completeness
