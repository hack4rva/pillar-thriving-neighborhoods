# continuity_planner

Purpose: Identify post-hackathon continuation opportunities and next steps.

## When To Use
- After demo or when a team wants to continue.

## Inputs
- MVP state, partners/contacts, research artifacts, and known constraints.

## Outputs
- Candidate partner orgs and points of contact.
- Artifacts to preserve (docs, data, code) and cleanup tasks.
- 30–60–90 day next steps and milestones.

## Process
1) Identify credible use cases and decision‑makers to engage.
2) Map needed artifacts and technical hardening tasks.
3) Propose a lightweight roadmap with risks and dependencies.
