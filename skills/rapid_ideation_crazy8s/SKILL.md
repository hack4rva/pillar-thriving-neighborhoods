# rapid_ideation_crazy8s

Purpose: Generate a high volume of ideas quickly to avoid early convergence.

## When To Use
- Team is stuck or slow to ideate
- Dominant voices controlling direction
- Need to break out of obvious solutions

## Inputs
- Problem statement

## Process
1. Divide a sheet/doc into 8 sections
2. Each participant generates 8 distinct ideas in 8 minutes
3. No discussion or critique during generation
4. Share all ideas with the group
5. Vote on top concepts (dot voting or thumbs)

## Outputs
- 8+ raw ideas per participant
- Top-voted concepts to carry forward

## Constraints
- No critique during ideation phase
- Speed over quality — volume is the goal
