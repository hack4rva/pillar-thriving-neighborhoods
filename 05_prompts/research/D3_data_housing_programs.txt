Data Inventory D3 — Publicly Accessible Housing Investment Data for Richmond

Inventory the publicly accessible data sources that document affordable housing investments in Richmond, including both City-administered and federally subsidized programs.

Cover:
- What Legistar contains related to housing funding: grant approvals, loan authorizations, affordability agreements approved by City Council
- How to search Legistar for housing-related ordinances and resolutions
- HUD Affordable Housing Preservation catalog: what properties it covers, how to filter for Richmond, field structure
- NLIHC National Housing Preservation Database: coverage, access method, reliability for Richmond
- HUD CHAS data: what it measures, how to access Richmond-specific tables, known limitations
- VHDA (Virginia Housing) data: what's publicly available for Richmond, access method
- Any other publicly accessible sources that document Richmond's affordable housing inventory

Output
- Facts (with URLs)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Unknowns
- Inventory table: source, coverage, access method, fields, limitations
- Which sources are most useful for a housing investment dashboard vs. a compliance monitoring tool
