Prior Art E5 — Weekend-Viable Civic Tech Projects in Housing and Planning

Identify civic tech projects in housing and planning transparency that were built in hackathon or sprint contexts (24–72 hours) and were successful enough to be continued or deployed.

Focus on:
- Projects that used Legistar, municipal GIS, or HUD open data as their core data source
- Projects built at Code for America hackathons, civic tech jams, or similar events
- What technical choices made them buildable in a weekend (static data, pre-fetched JSON, no real-time API dependency)
- What scope decisions made them demoable in 48 hours
- What made judges or partners value them
- Which ones continued beyond the hackathon and why

Output
- Facts (with URLs)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Unknowns
- Top 3 weekend-viable patterns directly applicable to Richmond Thriving Neighborhoods
- What a Richmond team should copy, adapt, or avoid based on this scan
