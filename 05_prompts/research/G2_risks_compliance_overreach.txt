Risks G2 — Risk of Compliance Determination Overreach

Analyze the specific risks of a housing compliance monitoring tool making or implying compliance determinations about funded developments.

Cover:
- What a "compliance determination" is and why it is a City official function, not a software function
- How a tool might inadvertently imply compliance or non-compliance: language choices, color coding (red/green flags), thresholds without context
- The legal and reputational consequences if a tool incorrectly flags a development as non-compliant
- How developer and City relationships could be damaged by a public-facing compliance tool
- What the difference is between "surfacing information for staff review" and "making a compliance determination"
- Required disclaimer language for any compliance-adjacent tool
- How to design a staff-support tool that helps without overreaching

Output
- Risk assessment with severity and likelihood
- Language guardrails: what words and UI patterns to avoid
- Mitigations for each overreach risk
- Safe framing: how to present compliance-adjacent information without implying determinations
