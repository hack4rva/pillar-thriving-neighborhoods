Services Landscape C2 — Richmond Planning Commission Process, Timelines, and Notification Requirements

Describe the Richmond development review process in detail, focusing on the steps between when a development application is filed and when a decision is made — and specifically when and how residents are legally required to be notified.

Cover:
- What types of applications require Planning Commission review vs. administrative approval
- The typical timeline from application submission to Planning Commission hearing to City Council action
- Legal notification requirements: what triggers a mailed notice, what the mailing radius is, what must be posted on-site
- How and where agenda items are posted publicly: City website, Legistar, physical posting
- What "public comment period" means and when it opens and closes for typical development cases
- Whether Richmond sends digital notifications to residents or neighborhood associations

Output
- Facts (with URLs)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Unknowns
- Key deadlines in the process where a notification tool would be most valuable
- Gap analysis: where the legal notification process falls short of practical discoverability
