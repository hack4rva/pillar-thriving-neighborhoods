User & Stakeholder B2 — City Housing Staff Persona

Describe the likely persona for City housing staff responsible for monitoring compliance with affordable housing agreements in Richmond.

Include:
- Role description: what this person is responsible for, what their workload looks like, what their technical capabilities are
- Current workflow: what systems they use, what manual processes they do, where data is scattered
- Pain points: what takes the most time, what is hardest to verify, what they wish they had
- Constraints: what they cannot do (make compliance determinations unilaterally, share confidential data externally)
- What a helpful tool would look like from their perspective
- What would make a tool unhelpful or risky from their perspective

Consider that housing compliance staff typically:
- Work across a portfolio of 10–25+ funded developments
- Must verify rent levels, income restrictions, and reporting requirements
- Rely on developer self-reports supplemented by spot checks
- Have limited staff capacity and budget

Output
- 1–2 staff personas with goals, workflow, pain points, and tool requirements
- What success looks like from a staff perspective
- What the minimum viable version of a helpful tool would be
