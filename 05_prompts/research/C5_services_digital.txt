Services Landscape C5 — Digital Planning Tools in Richmond

Inventory the digital tools Richmond currently provides for residents and developers related to land use, development, and planning.

Cover:
- The Online Permit Portal (OPP): what it contains, who uses it, what development information is available
- The Richmond GeoHub: specific planning-related layers, how the public accesses them, known limitations
- The City Planning Department website: what development-related information is posted, how it is organized
- Any existing digital tools for searching zoning, permits, or development activity
- Whether Richmond has a development notification subscription system
- Gap between what digital tools exist and what residents actually need

Output
- Facts (with URLs to each tool)
- Inferences (clearly labeled)
- Unknowns
- Assessment of which existing tools could be extended or complemented by a hackathon prototype
- Recommendations for which tools a team should build on vs. build alongside
